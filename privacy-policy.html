Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 19, 2026  ·  Shine for a Cause Mobile Detailing  ·  Fort Collins, Colorado

Shine for a Cause Mobile Detailing ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our booking system, or interact with our advertisements.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, phone number, email address, and service address when you book a service or submit a form.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, referring URLs, browser type, and device information collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Ad interaction data — information collected when you interact with our ads on platforms such as Meta (Facebook/Instagram), including form submissions through Meta Lead Ads.
  • Payment information — we do not collect or store payment information online. All payments are collected in person after service completion.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Schedule and confirm your detailing appointment
  • Contact you about your booking, quote, or inquiry
  • Send service reminders or follow-up messages (only with your consent)
  • Improve our website, services, and marketing
  • Run and measure the performance of our advertising campaigns
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Advertising

We use Meta's advertising platform to run ads on Facebook and Instagram. When you interact with our ads or submit a lead form on Meta, your information may be shared with us directly through Meta's Lead Ads system. This is governed by Meta's own Privacy Policy in addition to ours.

We may use the Meta Pixel on our website to track conversions and optimize ad delivery. The Pixel collects data such as page views and booking events. You can opt out of Meta's data collection through your Facebook ad preferences settings.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies, including Google Analytics, to understand how visitors use our site. This data is anonymous and aggregated. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly as a result.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers — such as our booking software (Acuity Scheduling) and analytics tools (Google Analytics) that help us operate our business.
  • Advertising platforms — Meta and Google, in connection with our advertising campaigns, subject to their respective privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or government authority.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Booking and contact records are typically kept for up to 2 years for business records purposes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as our booking system). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
