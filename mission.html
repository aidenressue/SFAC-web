Our Mission

More Than
a Detail

We started Shine for a Cause because we believe a business can be a force for good in its community. Every time you book with us — whether it's a quick exterior wash or a full ceramic coating — you're part of that mission.

We donate a portion of every job to organizations and individuals in Northern Colorado fighting for sobriety, second chances, and a better life. No red tape, no corporate giving programs. Just real dollars going to real people in our backyard.

Who We Support

The Cause

Addiction touches nearly every family in our community. We support the organizations and programs doing the hard work of recovery every single day.

🤝

Local AA & NA Groups

We donate directly to local Alcoholics Anonymous and Narcotics Anonymous chapters — funding meetings, literature, and resources for people in early recovery.

🏠

SAFE Foundation

The SAFE Foundation provides sober living, counseling, and wraparound support for individuals rebuilding their lives. We're proud to be a recurring contributor.

🌱

Individual Sponsorships

Sometimes the most impactful thing is funding one person's treatment. We directly sponsor individuals through rehab and treatment programs right here in Northern Colorado.

We're not just a detailing company with a donation box. The cause is built into how we operate — into every estimate we send, every driveway we pull up to, and every handshake at the end of a job.
